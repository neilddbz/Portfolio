About Me

I am a Computer Engineering graduate with professional experience in IT Support, Network Operations, and Network Engineering. My career progression from technical support to NOC and field network deployment has equipped me with strong troubleshooting skills, infrastructure knowledge, and real-world operational experience.

I started in IT Support, handling hardware diagnostics, system setup, cabling, CCTV infrastructure, wireless configuration, and technical troubleshooting. This foundation strengthened my ability to resolve technical issues efficiently and support end-users in live environments.

As a NOC / Network Engineer, I perform 24/7 network monitoring, incident management, bandwidth optimization, firewall and VPN configuration, and real-time troubleshooting of packet loss, latency, and connectivity issues. I also conduct site surveys, deploy wireless Point-to-Point (PtP) and Point-to-Multipoint (PtMP) systems, install and align radios, and ensure stable, secure network performance for enterprise and government clients.

I am committed to maintaining reliable, secure, and high-performance network infrastructure while continuously improving operational efficiency and minimizing downtime.
