About Me

I am a Computer Engineering graduate with professional experience in Network Engineering, NOC Operations, and IT Infrastructure Deployment. I have designed, deployed, and maintained LAN/WAN networks, wireless PtP/PtMP systems, and enterprise-grade connectivity solutions for clients including government agencies, BPOs, hospitals, and corporate establishments.

I specialize in network monitoring, troubleshooting packet loss and latency, configuring firewalls and VPNs, and optimizing bandwidth. My work includes conducting site surveys and installing wireless radios on towers and poles, aligning them for stable, high-performance connectivity. With hands-on experience in structured cabling, PoE setups, and on-site troubleshooting, I am committed to delivering secure, reliable, and optimized networks while minimizing downtime.
