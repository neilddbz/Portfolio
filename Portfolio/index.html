About Me

I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering from Lyceum of Alabang (July 2023) and completed Senior High School (ICT Strand) in April 2019 with an NC II in Computer Systems Servicing.

My early exposure to IT started with my Work Immersion at Creotec Philippines Inc. (December 2018), working in the EMS Assembly Line Process, focusing on soldering, circuit board repair, and board preparation.

In college, I completed my OJT at The Seashore Beach Club, Inc. (April 2023 - June 2023) as an IT Support Intern. I developed the company website, installed CCTV systems, configured Tenda devices, and assisted in fiber-optic and UTP cable installations.

Most recently, I worked as a Field Service Engineer / ATM Technician at One Point Contact Inc. (February 2024 - May 2025), servicing ATMs for BDO, Metrobank, PSBank, EastWest Bank, PNB, PBCOM, and 7-Eleven.

With strong foundations in IT support, networking, and systems maintenance, I am eager to contribute my skills to a forward-thinking IT role.
